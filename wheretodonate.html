Where to donate blood


Giving blood

You can give blood across Nasipit, from permanent donor Clinics and Hospital to pop-up community venues.

More about giving blood:
  • A blood donation is taken from your arm.
  • The appointment takes around an hour in total, including 10 to 15 minutes to donate, plus time to have a drink and a snack.

About our donation venues

We run most of our appointments at 27 main donor centres where we have permanent teams and greatest capacity. Right now, there are spaces we must fill in order to maintain a good blood supply to hospitals and clinics.
