Upcoming Blood Donation Campaigns in Nasipit

"Save a Life, Give Blood" - Nasipit District Hospital

Location: Nasipit District Hospital, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte

Date: October 10, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Organizers: Nasipit District Hospital & Red Cross Agusan Chapter

Perks & Incentives: Free medical check-up, donor certificates, snacks, and a chance to win a wellness kit.

Additional Details: This campaign aims to collect 100 blood bags to support emergency cases. First 50 donors will receive a free blood pressure monitoring kit. Community volunteers will be available to assist donors. Blood donation awareness talks will be held every hour.

"Give Blood, Give Hope" - Nasipit Community Blood Drive


Location: Nasipit Municipal Hall, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte

Date: November 15, 2025

Time: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Organizers: Philippine Blood Coordinating Council (PBCC) & Local Government of Nasipit

Perks & Incentives: Free health screening, donor recognition certificates, refreshments, and a chance to win a fitness tracker. The first 75 donors will receive a complimentary gift bag.

Additional Details: This campaign targets collecting 150 blood units to enhance the local blood supply for medical emergencies. Blood donation education sessions will be conducted throughout the event. Community volunteers will be on hand to assist donors and ensure a smooth experience.
